Who am i?

I'm Yiting Wang, an Advanced Product Engineer and a Research Intern

I'm currently working in the visualization team of Siemens Industrial Software Company developing NX in C++(Please check this link Siemens NX for further details of this software). During my part-time, I research multimodal and transfer learning with electroencephalogram(EEG) and eye movement signals for emotion recognition. Combining different types of features including time-series (temporal features) and topological structure (spacial features) of EEG signals for emotion recognition is also my focus right now. My interests are not limited to Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) but extend to other fields of Human-Computer Interface (HCI) like smart devices, wearable devices, intelligent user interfaces, etc.
