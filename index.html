Yiting Wang

I am a PhD student at the Eye movement and Vision Lab , Karolinska Insitutet (KI) supervied by Dr.Mattias Nilsson and Prof.Tony Pansell. My research interests lie at the intersection of deep learning algorithm, multimodal learning and human physiological signals. I'm currently working on identifying biomarker of fixational eye movements for early-stage Parkinson's Disease with machine learning.

My Background is Electrical and Computer Engineering. Before starting the PhD, I did the research work about recognizing emotion states with electroencephalogram (EEG) and eye movement data at BCMI Lab adviced by Prof.Bao-Liang Lu. Besides the research experience, I worked in Siemens Industry Software developing software NX in C++. I finished the master at Cornell University and the bachelor at Shanghai University.
