Emotion recognition, a fundamental constitution of Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCIs), plays an important role in diagnosing and treating many mental disorders as well as affective computing.

Brain-Heart Machine Interface

My MEng project was part of Close-loop Brain-Heart Machine Interface, which intends to design a real time system that combines heart rate with EEG to reflect human emotion and regulate it with external interventions like music and blinkding Led lights.
