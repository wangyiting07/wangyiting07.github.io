Who am i?

I'm Yiting Wang, an Advanced Product Engineer and a Research Intern

I'm currently working in visualization team of Siemens Industrial Software Company developing NX in C++(Please check this link Siemens NX for further details of this software). During my part time, I study on multimodal and transfer learning with electroencephalogram(EEG) and eye movement signals for emotion recognition. Combining different types of features including time series and topological structure of EEG signals for emotion recognition is also my focus right now. My interests are not limited to Brain-Computer Interface (BCI), but extend to other fields of Human-Computer Interface (HCI) like smart devices, wearable devices, inteligent user interface, etc.
